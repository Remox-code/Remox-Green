Plant Care Tips

Keep your plants happy with these essential care recommendations

Light

Make sure each plant gets the right amount of natural or indirect light based on its type.

Water

Avoid overwatering. Check the soil moisture before watering to prevent root damage.

Temperature

Keep plants away from extreme heat, cold drafts, and sudden temperature changes.

Maintenance

Trim dead leaves, clean the foliage, and repot when necessary to support healthy growth.
